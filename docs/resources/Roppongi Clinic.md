Offers mental health counseling as well as diagnosis for ADHD/Autism.

Does not provide HRT or hormone blood testing.

Some trans-friendly staff, but not guaranteed. The clinic does require you to fill out your legal name/gender on forms, without an option to provide preferred name, but some counselors will refer to you by preferred name.
